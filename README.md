# ScholarNet

An academic research and knowledge sharing rewards platform built on Stacks blockchain using Clarity smart contracts.

## Overview

ScholarNet enables researchers and academics to track their research contributions, earn scholarship rewards, and participate in a decentralized knowledge sharing ecosystem. The platform promotes academic excellence through transparent contribution tracking.

## Features

- Academic field validation and tracking
- Knowledge credit accumulation system
- Scholarship-based reward distribution
- Research director management
- Transparent peer review system

## Smart Contract Functions

### Public Functions
- `establish-scholar-network` - Initialize research network with director
- `validate-academic-field` - Validate academic fields for tracking
- `record-research-contribution` - Record academic research progress
- `conduct-peer-review-assessment` - Calculate and distribute scholarship rewards
- `complete-scholarly-certification` - Complete certification and claim rewards

### Read-Only Functions
- `get-research-contributions` - Get scholar's research progress
- `get-academic-field` - Get scholar's field specialization
- `get-total-knowledge-credits` - Get total platform knowledge credits
- `is-field-validated` - Check if field is validated for tracking

## Getting Started

1. Deploy the contract to Stacks blockchain
2. Establish scholar network with research director
3. Validate academic fields for tracking
4. Begin recording research contributions
5. Conduct peer reviews and claim scholarship rewards
